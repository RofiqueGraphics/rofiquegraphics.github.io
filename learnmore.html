         History of Kilburn

Kilburn is an area of northwest London, England, located 3.75 miles north-west of Charing Cross. A small section of the area is located within the City of Westminster The town of Kilburn has its origins in a 12th-century priory on the banks of the Kilburn Brook. Kilburn today is a vibrant area and multicultural London district. Furthermore it is one of the capital's highest Irish populations, as well as a sizable Afro-Caribbean population. The area is one of 35 major centres in Greater London
