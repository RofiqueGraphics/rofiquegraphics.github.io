Activities Scenery

         Activities

Paddington recreation has a huge variety of activities to do such as playing sports like football, basketball, tennis, running or instead of participating in hectic sports you are able to relax by sitting in the café and have a warm drink. If this is not enough, Paddington Recreation also provides a gym for those that are conscious about their health or a park that focuses on younger children. Whatever your needs are the entire park provides it while being an enjoying place to visit.


         Scenery

This park has some un-imaginable scenery such as the peaceful nature walks or the busy football courts. The nature walks are normally not busy, therefore you be able to stroll at a steady pace and gaze upon the different creatures, ponds and flowers. It also has a huge area of land where you can walk around and admire the flowers or take your dog out to play. Furthermore, there is a location where you are able to talk and eat while you see the greenery of the park.
