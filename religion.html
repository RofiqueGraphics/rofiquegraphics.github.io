Islam

Some information about Islam is that they believe in one God, Allah, and the Prophet Muhammed (SAW) was Allah's last prophet. Islam means peace and Muslim people aim to live their life the same way the prophet did by following the five pillars of Islam which is shahada (confession of faith), prayer, zakat, fasting, and Hajj.

This is one of the most common religions that people follow in this diverse area. It is home to an amazing Muslim mosque, which is located near the start of the Kilburn road. It is very beautiful and modern; Islam is a religion of Peace and therefore suits the area as many people are friendly and are open to many beliefs. Living as a Muslim in Kilburn will be easy due to the variety of regular Islamic events and the population of Muslims in the area.

    Christianity

This is another common religion people believe in in the area. It also has a famous area to worship such as the church that is located near St. Augustine's High School. The church appears to be very old fashioned but the interior shows that it is a beautiful place to worship. Being a Christian and settling into Kilburn is very easy due to the similarity of people that follow the religion. Also due to the areas open mind.

    Demographic

Kilburn has an abundance of ethnic groups, including people of Irish, Afro Caribbean, Indian, Bangladeshi, Pakistani, Eritrean and Ethiopian background. It is also home to many religions, genders and sexuality. Some very common religions are Islam, Christianity, Hinduism and Judaism. This outlines how diverse and multi-cultural the area is and indicates that all people of different religions are welcome. Kilburn also has many places of worship.
